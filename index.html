How does spin wheel randomly pick items?

Spin Wheel Maker is completely unbias towards any item placed within it. It will programmatically pick a number between 1 and however many items you have on the wheel. Each slice of the wheel is programmed to have a number and will land on the number that was picked by the computer.

Since the spinner is all done by the computer, you can have the guarantee that it will remove any bias from your decision making. You simple load up your items and spin the wheel to decide your fate!

How does spin wheel randomly pick items?

Spin Wheel Maker is completely unbias towards any item placed within it. It will programmatically pick a number between 1 and however many items you have on the wheel. Each slice of the wheel is programmed to have a number and will land on the number that was picked by the computer.

Since the spinner is all done by the computer, you can have the guarantee that it will remove any bias from your decision making. You simple load up your items and spin the wheel to decide your fate!

How do use spin wheel maker

Spin Wheel Maker is a free to use tool that can be used to generate a random wheel on the fly. The best part is that it is super easy to use!

You will first want to enter an item into the input field. You can either click the button or simply press enter in order for the new item to appear on the wheel.

The wheel will update as each item is added or deleted. You can add as many options as you want. The more items the better!

If you ever want to rearrange the order in which the items are positioned. You can simply grab it by pressing right click on your mouse. As you hold the button, you will be giving the option to drag and drop it in the order that you want.

As you rearrange the order, the wheel itself will update with that order. This will prevent you from having to delete items and go back in order to get a specific order.

When you are ready to spin the wheel, you can simply click spin wheel button or just click on the wheel itself. It will spin a few times and announce the winner on the popup.

To close the popup, you can either click on the button or the grey background. When you close the popup, it will reset the wheel it's original position.
